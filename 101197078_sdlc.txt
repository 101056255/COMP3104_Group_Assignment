Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)

What is SDLC?

SDLC is reffered to as the application development life cycle which is a process
for planning, creating, testing and deploying an information system. There are usually
6 stages in the cycle which include requirment analysis, design, development and testing,
implementation, documentation and evaluation.

Importance of Devops in SDLC
- Development and operations work closely to accelerate innovation
- Devops ensures higher quality software can be devoloped more quickly and with more reliability
- Updates to products are small but frequent
- Continuous feedback and process improvement
- automation of manual development processs

All these facts show how Devops can create an efficient, reliable and higher quality model for software development.
