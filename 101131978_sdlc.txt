what is devops
DevOps is a software development method in which the development team
can cooperate with operations/functional departments at all stages of
software development. This includes product design, development, testing,
debugging and support.

one of the benefits of learning devops

	-Increase Deployment Success Rates
		Programming errors are one of the main reasons for
		deployment failures. Frequent code releases caused
		by DevOps methods can ensure that problems are found
		at an early stage. With the joint efforts of the
		developers and the operations team, the recovery time
		has been greatly shortened
